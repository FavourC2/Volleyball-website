How do I sign up for club membership?

To sign up for club membership, please visit our You can sign up Here and fill out the registration form.

How much does club membership cost?

Club membership costs ¬£40 for the semester.

What are the benefits of club membership?

Club members have access to exclusive training sessions, club events, and discounts on equipment.

Can I join the club mid-semester?

Yes, you can join the club at any time during the semester. Membership fees will remain the same though.

Is there a trial period for club membership?

Yes, we offer a one-week trial period for new members. During this time, you can attend club events and training sessions before deciding to become a full member.
